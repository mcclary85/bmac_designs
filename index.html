My Backstory...

After working in the field of education for more than a decade, I decided it was time to chase a new horizon. I had a really cool business idea, but was unsure where to start. After consulting with several software engineers, and getting quotes that would require a second mortgage, I decided to just build it myself! The project is still in flight and should be launching soon. Stay tuned!

Anyways, after learning the basics I picked up a few freelance consulting and design jobs for local businesses. Working directly with business owners and helping them bring their visions to life is definitely my newly discovered passion. You can check out a few of my projects below.
