Our Story

My name is Brian. After working in the field of education for more than a decade, I determined it was time to chase a new horizon. I made the jump to a product consultant role at a SaaS company. This allowed me to work remotely, and provided me the opportunity to work side-by-side with tons of small and medium-sized business owners. I quickly realized most of my clients needed help with their brand identity and digital presence. As a result, BMAC Designs was born.

Our primary focus is to help small and medium-sized business owners reach the next level. Whether you are a new start-up looking for web design services, or if you are an established business looking for a brand refresh, we got you covered!
